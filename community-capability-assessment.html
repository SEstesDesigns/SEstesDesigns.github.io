Discovering where your strengths meet your community's needs

What do you bring to your community?

Your group has chosen hunger and food insecurity as your area of focus. This assessment helps you identify the strengths and preferences you each bring to that work — so you can figure out who does what, where the gaps are, and how to grow together.

An important starting point: People in your community are already working on hunger and food insecurity. Before you interpret your results, connect with them. Their experience will shape how you read your strengths, which roles actually matter, and where your team can contribute without duplicating what already exists. This assessment enhances those conversations — it doesn't replace them.

You'll rate yourself across six dimensions of community contribution, then tell us which areas you most enjoy. Every team needs a mix — no single person needs to be strong in all six.
